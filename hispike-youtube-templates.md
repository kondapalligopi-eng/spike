## Title formulas

Keyword first, brand at the end, under ~60 characters.

| Video type | Formula | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Local list | `Top 5 [thing] in Bengaluru (2026) \| HiSpike` | `Top 5 Dog Parks in Bengaluru (2026) \| HiSpike` |
| Area pick | `Best [service] in [area] — HiSpike Picks` | `Best Vets in Indiranagar — HiSpike Picks` |
| How-to / tips | `How to [do X] \| HiSpike Pet Care` | `How to Groom a Lab at Home \| HiSpike Pet Care` |
| Product | `[Product] Review — Worth It? \| HiSpike` | `Royal Canin vs Pedigree — Worth It? \| HiSpike` |
| Feature demo | `[Benefit] in 60 seconds 🐾 \| HiSpike` | `Give Your Dog Their Own Page in 60s 🐾 \| HiSpike` |

Rules: front-load the search term, add the year for "best/top" lists, keep it honest (no clickbait).
