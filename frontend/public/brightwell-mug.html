Print tips:

  • The dashed line is the safe area — keep all artwork inside it. The outer 60px is bleed and may get cut.
  • Single colour (emerald) by design — single-ink prints are cleaner, cheaper, and age better.
  • The artwork is centred on the wrap so it shows correctly with the handle on either side.
  • If your printer wants a different size, the PNG resizes without quality loss (it's 2475 × 1050 at 300 DPI — comfortable headroom).
