Print tips:

  • The dashed line is the safe area — keep all text inside it. Anything in the outer 60px (the bleed) may get cut by the printer.
  • The downloaded PNG has a transparent background, so the design prints cleanly on whatever mug colour you choose.
  • Most online services accept 8.25" × 3.5" at 300 DPI. If yours wants a different size, you can resize the PNG without quality loss (it's 2475 × 1050 — comfortably high-res).
  • For a single sided design (visible when right-handed users hold the handle on the right), tell the printer to centre the artwork. The wrap is symmetric.
